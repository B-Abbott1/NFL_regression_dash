ROLE Z — share / primary volume

- QB: dropbacks (vol_dropbacks)

- RB: opportunity_share

- WR/TE: opportunity_share



EFFICIENCY Z — process only (2–3 metrics; age NOT here)

- QB: qb_cpoe, qb_epa_per_play

- RB: rb_ryoe, rb_efficiency

- WR/TE: recv_ypt (70%), recv_separation (15%), recv_yac_oe (15%)



PRODUCTION Z — counting stats

- QB: passing_yards, passing_tds (INT excluded from production axis; was compressing elite volume)

- RB: scrimmage_yards, total_tds

- WR/TE: receiving_yards, receptions, receiving_tds



TAGS — edit cutoffs in config.py (TAG_* constants)

Uses role_z, efficiency_z, production_z (weighted mean when SCORE_AXIS_WEIGHTS set, else equal mean).

Baseline: prior season, position peak-age cohort. Leaderboard percentile = within-position rank of axis z.



Defaults (TAG_AXIS_HIGH_Z=1.5, TAG_AXIS_LOW_Z=-0.5):

- star:              prod_z >= 1.5 AND eff_z >= 1.5 (no age gate)

- regress_negative:  (prod_z >= 1.5 AND eff_z <= -0.5)

                     OR (role_z >= 1.5 AND prod_z <= -0.5)

                     OR (prod_z >= 1.5 AND eff_z <= 0 AND td_rate_z >= 1.5)

- breakout:          role_z <= -0.5 AND eff_z >= 1.5 AND qualified

- positive_outlook:  prod_z <= -0.5 AND eff_z >= 1.5

- neutral:           else



Age does NOT gate tags. It adjusts efficiency_z only:
  efficiency_z = mean(efficiency metric z) + efficiency_age_z_adjustment
  adjustment = -(age_curve_value) * EFFICIENCY_AGE_Z_PER_YEAR * position_weight
  (young → negative age_curve_value → boost eff z; old → penalty)

Qualification (breakout only): QB 100 dropbacks, WR/TE 40 targets, RB 60 carries.
