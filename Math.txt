ROLE Z (Rz) — share / primary volume

- QB: dropbacks (vol_dropbacks)
- RB: opportunity_share
- WR/TE: opportunity_share

EFFICIENCY Z (Ez) — process only (age adjustment applied to Ez)

- QB: qb_cpoe, qb_epa_per_play, qb_passer_rating, qb_anya
- RB: rb_ryoe, rb_efficiency
- WR/TE: recv_ypt (70%), recv_separation (15%), recv_yac_oe (15%)

PRODUCTION Z (Pz) — counting stats

- QB: passing_yards, passing_tds
- RB: scrimmage_yards, total_tds
- WR/TE: receiving_yards, receptions, receiving_tds

TAGS — within-position percentiles of Rz / Ez / Pz (see New_scoring.txt, config.TAG_*_PCT)

Priority: star > regress_negative > breakout > positive_outlook > neutral

- star:              Ez > 85th AND Pz > 90th
- regress_negative:  Pz > 60th AND Rz > 60th AND Ez < 35th
- breakout:          Rz < 50th AND Ez > 85th (volume qualified)
- positive_outlook:  Rz > 50th AND Ez > 85th
- neutral:           else

Leaderboard display percentile = same cohort rank for the selected focus axis.

Implemented: METRICS_SCORING_VERSION=14.
